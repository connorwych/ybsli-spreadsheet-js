Fees

Freelance interpreters will have a minimum fee for their work. This will vary from person to person, please ask when you are making a booking.

The cost payable depends on a number of factors including:

  • The type of the assignment (e.g. Legal work is typically more expensive)
  • The times and duration of the assignment.
  • The qualifications and experience of the interpreter.
  • The mileage/travel costs.

Longer assignments may need a second interpreter to be booked in order to co-work. You will receive professional advice from whoever you are in contact with.

Please be aware that cancellation fees are often applicable. Please check with the interpreter when discussing the booking.

An indication of a range of typical fees charged can be found on the ASLI website (http://www.asli.org.uk/fees-salaries-report-p122.aspx)
