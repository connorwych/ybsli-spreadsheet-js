Qualifications

Interpreters listed on this website are fully qualified BSL/English Interpreters. RSLI after their name refers to a member of the Register of Sign Language Interpreters, held by the NRCPD (National Register of Communication Professionals working with Deaf and Deafblind People).

These interpreters have agreed to abide by a code of conduct and complaints procedure. They will have professional indemnity insurance and identification cards. RSLIâ€™s will also have a current enhanced DBS certificate available for you to check. RSLI

MVLP refers to a member of Visual Language Professionals. Members carry an ID card which clearly states that they are qualified. VLP

MASLI refers to a Full Member of the Association of Sign Language Interpreters ASLI.

*Qualifications are:

  • Postgraduate qualification in BSL/English interpreting (PGDIP or MA)
  • Signature Level 6 NVQ in Interpreting BSL/English (including Signature Level 4 NVQ BSL/English interpreting)
  • CACDP RQI exam (pre NVQ)

About

This website is a quick and convenient way for you to contact qualified, registered British Sign Language interpreters who live locally.
